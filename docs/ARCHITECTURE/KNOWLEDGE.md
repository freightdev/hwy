# Knowledge Architecture

HWY should improve through evidence, not magical self-modification.

## Knowledge loop

Flow execution creates telemetry.

Telemetry feeds Legal Logger.

Legal Logger writes Flow Reports.

Flow Reports update Flow Profiles.

Flow Profiles reveal patterns.

Big Bear may recommend improvements.

Humans approve structural changes.

## Rule

Do not let flows silently rewrite themselves.

Do not treat memory as trusted by default.

Important knowledge should be source-backed, logbook-backed, or explicitly labeled as uncertain.
